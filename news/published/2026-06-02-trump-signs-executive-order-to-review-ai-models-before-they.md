The executive order signed by President Trump establishes a voluntary framework for AI companies to share their advanced AI models with the US government before public release, aiming to enhance security and protect critical infrastructure. This policy development impacts how AI models, including those like Claude Code, may be reviewed and regulated prior to deployment.
