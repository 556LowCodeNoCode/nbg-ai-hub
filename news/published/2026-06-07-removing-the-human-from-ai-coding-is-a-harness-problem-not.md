This post discusses the challenge of removing humans from AI coding workflows, emphasizing that the key issue is building better harnesses around AI agents rather than improving the models themselves. It advocates for rigorous, spec-driven frameworks, programmatic verification, and maintaining audit trails to safely automate coding tasks while keeping humans in the loop where necessary.
